How to Use TypeRacer
  1. Select your desired difficulty level from the dropdown menu.
  2. A sample sentence will appear. Start typing in the text box to begin the test.
  3. Correctly typed words will be highlighted in blue, incorrect in red.
  4. The timer starts automatically when you begin typing.
  5. Press Enter to finish the test and see your results.
  6. Click "Retry" to try a new sentence of the same difficulty.
